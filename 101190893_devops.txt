This file will contain information about COMP 3104 DevOps course
 
COMP3104 is a DevOps course that will provide a hands-on experience of through the implementation of tried, tested and true DevOps technologies. The course is presented by Pritesh Patel. 

With the high-level overview of what DevOps is, the course also unpacks some commonly used terms and introduce ourselves to industry standard and tools that are often used in the field.
